The Search for My Computational Programming Language

Execution vs Development Time

Having used R for 3 years in college, I've gotten to know the limits and conveniences of R. As a statistical package, with a large support community it serves its purpose well. However, at times, especially when doing more computation-heavy analysis, R slows down, and reaches memory limits. Hence, I've been looking around for a language that is reasonalbly fast, and quick to develop. I checked out C, C++, Go, Scala, and Julia.
